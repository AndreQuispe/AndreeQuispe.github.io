Data Cleaning and Anonymization of Health Center Patient Data

This project focuses on demonstrating proficiency in Python for data cleaning tasks. The dataset comprises admissions data from 18 health centers in the Cusco region over a span of four years (2019 - 2022), specifically for patients with high blood pressure and/or diabetes.

Key Steps:

  • Data Compilation: The initial task involved gathering data from multiple sources, consolidating them into a single file for streamlined analysis.
  • Addressing Data Inconsistencies: Significant effort was devoted to handling inconsistencies in patient records. Issues such as variations in patient names across entries and discrepancies in identity document numbers were tackled.
  • Data Cleaning: Extensive data cleaning procedures were employed to rectify inconsistencies and errors within the dataset. Techniques such as standardization of patient names and identity document numbers were applied to enhance data quality and coherence.
  • Anonymization of Patient Data: To uphold privacy standards and compliance regulations, the final step involved anonymizing patient information.
